Japanese Sentence Analysis

Sentence: 太りたくなければ、夜遅く食べないことです。

Translation: "If you don't want to get fat, do not eat late at night."

Grammar Breakdown:

  • 太りたくなければ (futoritakunakereba): If you don't want to get fat
  • 夜遅く (yoru osoku): Late at night
  • 食べないこと (tabenai koto): The act of not eating
  • です (desu): Polite ending particle
