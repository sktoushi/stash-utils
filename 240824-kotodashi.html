Japanese Sentence Analysis

来週はお客様が来ることだし、家の中の大掃除をしなくちゃ。

Translation: "Since guests are coming next week, I must do a thorough cleaning of the house."

Grammar Breakdown

  • 来週 (らいしゅう) [raishuu]: "Next week."
  • お客様 (おきゃくさま) [okyakusama]: "Guest" or "customer." The respectful suffix "様 (さま)" is used.
  • が来る (がくる) [ga kuru]: "Will come." The verb 来る (くる) [kuru] means "to come."
  • ことだし [koto dashi]: A colloquial expression used to indicate a reason or cause.
  • 家の中 (いえのなか) [ie no naka]: "Inside the house."
  • 大掃除 (おおそうじ) [oosouji]: "Big cleaning" or "spring cleaning."
  • をしなくちゃ [wo shinakucha]: A contraction of しなければならない [shinakereba naranai], meaning "must do."
