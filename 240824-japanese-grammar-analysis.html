Japanese Grammar Analysis

The sentence provided is: とても〜ので、普通ではない結果になってしまった。

Translation: "Because it was so [extreme], it resulted in an abnormal outcome."

Grammar Breakdown:

  • とても〜ので (totemo ~node): Used to express that something happened because of an extreme condition.
  • 普通ではない (futsuu dewa nai): Means "not normal" or "abnormal".
  • 結果 (kekka): Means "result".
  • なってしまった (natte shimatta): Indicates that something happened unintentionally or with a negative nuance.

This sentence demonstrates the usage of "あまり・あまりの～に" which is used to express that something happens as a result of being too much or too extreme.
